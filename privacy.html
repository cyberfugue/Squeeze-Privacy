Privacy Policy for Squeeze

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

Squeeze ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Progress Data: Locally stored on your device including achievements, unlocks, and in-game currency
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting
  • Ad Interaction Data: Anonymous data about ad views and interactions (collected by AdMob)

Information You Provide

  • In-App Purchases: Transaction information processed through Apple's App Store (we do not store payment information)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple's payment system
  • Analyze crashes and errors to improve app stability
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All game progress is stored locally on your device using browser/app local storage
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal information on external servers
  • Game Data: Can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

  • Purpose: To display advertisements
  • Data Collected: Device identifier, IP address, ad interaction data
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-Out: You can purchase ad removal or use iOS ad tracking settings

Sentry

  • Purpose: Error and crash reporting
  • Data Collected: Device information, app version, error logs
  • Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

Apple App Store

  • Purpose: In-app purchase processing
  • Data Collected: Transaction information
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Advertising

We display third-party advertisements through Google AdMob. Advertisers may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Display personalized ads based on your interests
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Understand user behavior

You can opt-out of personalized ads through:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Purchasing our "Remove Ads" in-app purchase ($4.99)

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple's payment system
  • We do not collect or store sensitive personal information

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your locally stored game data
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads through iOS settings
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the app at any time

Data Retention

  • Game Progress: Stored indefinitely on your device until you delete the app
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google AdMob per their retention policies

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notification
  • Posting the new policy in the app and on our website

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

  • Legitimate Interests: To provide game functionality and improve user experience
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws

Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.
