Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the in-game shop and tap the "Restore Purchases" button. This will restore any previous purchases including ad removal. Make sure you're logged into the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.
I lost my game progress. Can I get it back?
Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted the app or cleared your data, unfortunately the progress cannot be recovered. We recommend backing up your device regularly through iCloud to prevent data loss.
How do I remove ads?
You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" option in the in-game shop for $4.99. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all advertisements from the game.
The game is crashing or freezing. What should I do?
Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Close the app completely and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
  4. Ensure your iOS is up to date
  5. If the problem persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version
How do I unlock new levels or features?
Progress through the game by completing levels and earning in-game currency. You can unlock new tools, skins, and levels by spending your earned coins. Some premium features are available through in-app purchases.
Is there cloud save or sync between devices?
Currently, Squeeze stores all game data locally on your device. Cloud save functionality is not available at this time. Each device maintains its own separate progress.
Can I play offline?
Yes! Squeeze can be played completely offline. However, ads require an internet connection to load. If you've purchased ad removal, you can enjoy the game without any internet connection.

Refund Policy

All in-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Refund requests must be made through Apple:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase you want to request a refund for
  4. Select "Report a Problem" and follow the prompts

Apple's refund policy applies to all purchases. We cannot process refunds directly.
